About This Crossword Solver

This crossword solver is a fast, free tool designed to help you solve crosswords, word puzzles, and anagrams with minimal friction.

Whether you are stuck on a single clue or working through a full grid, the app gives you practical ways to find the right word quickly—without clutter, paywalls, or unnecessary features.

Solve Crossword Clues by Pattern

At its core, the solver helps you find words that match a specific letter pattern. You can enter known letters and leave gaps for unknown ones, making it easy to solve classic crossword clues like:

  • _ A _ _ E R
  • C _ O S S _ O R D
  • Words of a fixed length with partial information

Results are returned instantly, so you can explore possibilities and move on without breaking your flow.

Powerful Anagram Solver (With Known Letters)

The app also includes a flexible anagram solver. You can:

  • Find all anagrams of a set of letters
  • Solve partial anagrams when some letters are already fixed
  • Narrow results by word length or letter position

This makes it useful not just for crosswords, but also for cryptic clues, word games, and general wordplay.

Built-In Thesaurus and Synonym Search

A comprehensive thesaurus is integrated directly into the solver. You can look up synonyms by meaning, helping you answer clues like:

  • “Alternative to…”
  • “Similar to…”
  • “Informally meaning…”

This avoids jumping between multiple sites and keeps everything in one place.

Integrated Dictionary Lookups

Each word is backed by dictionary data, so you can quickly check definitions and confirm meanings as you solve. This is especially useful for:

  • Verifying uncommon or archaic words
  • Understanding cryptic crossword answers
  • Learning new vocabulary as you go

Fast, Simple, and Free

The solver is built to be snappy and easy to use:

  • No sign-up
  • No limits
  • No ads getting in the way

It works instantly in your browser and is fully free for everyone.

If you enjoy crosswords and word puzzles and want a straightforward tool that just works, this solver is here to help.
